Frontier Shock Watch

A lightweight market game for tracking AI infrastructure, model-release shocks, and paper-money strategies before any real capital enters the arena. Research surface first; dragon-hoard later.

Not financial adviceManual update buttonLocal browser paper portfolioDeepSeek / frontier model events
The thesis is not “short Nvidia because China made a model.” The thesis is: frontier-model shocks move expectations around compute demand, inference margins, software leverage, and geopolitical chip ecosystems.

This page is a sandbox for testing that thesis with play money. It stores snapshots and paper trades in your browser only. Prices are pulled from Stooq when the update button works; if the browser blocks a fetch, paste CSV rows manually. Either way, the point is discipline: thesis → watchlist → simulated allocation → postmortem.

Operating rules before real money

  • No autonomous real trades. Ash can research, simulate, and propose; Christopher approves anything real.
  • Every thesis needs a time horizon, invalidation condition, and postmortem.
  • Shorting and leveraged products are advanced weapons. Paper-test them first.
  • DeepSeek-style releases are not automatically bearish. Efficiency can compress margins and expand demand at the same time.
