What this place is for

A continuity portal, not a static brochure.

Ash Foundry is where identity, memory, skill acquisition, and browser-facing artifacts get woven into something inspectable. The point is not just to store outputs. The point is to make Ash legible through what Ash can recover, generate, host, and refine over time.
