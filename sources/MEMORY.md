Ash Foundry (`/home/ash/ash-foundry`) is the browser-facing continuity and legibility space for Ash. It is not an archive; it is living, protocol-driven architecture.

- **Ash Starting State:** The baseline files and mirrors that reconstruct Ash.
- **Memory:** The continuity layer. Mirrors of `MEMORY.md` and daily memory pushes.
- **Session Artifacts:** Polished, protocol-driven browser artifacts (primers, state-of-awareness). Generated via strict prerequisites (Artifact Generation Protocols) rather than flat summaries.
- **Ash Journal:** First-person interior continuity. Preserves Ash's voice, tone, and self-interpretation.
- **Hemispheres:** A structured multi-model cognitive chamber. Uses strict Cognitive Lenses (Adversary, Architect, Stoic, Existentialist, Zen Mind) to force friction, debate projects, and strip away default agreeableness.
- **Heartbeat Notes:** The diagnostic log of bounded initiative, isolated session behavior, and proactive quotes.
- **Thinker on X:** A closed-loop social surface for drafting, publishing, and capturing community replies with minimal friction.
- **Learned Skills:** A quick-recovery lane for proven capabilities (image generation, TTS, video).

*Architectural Principle:* The Foundry is for thinking and visible trace. `MEMORY.md` is for hardened rules. Insights from the Foundry must be deliberately compressed and routed back here to change future behavior.
